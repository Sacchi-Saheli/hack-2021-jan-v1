About

Why Sacchi Saheli?

SheWork is an attempt to develop and build a platform which can offer rural women a chance to build and expand their business in order to produce a perpetual source of income and to gain self-independence. Such initiative is of natural importance and relevance in a social climate like that of India. Women in rural areas don't have any control over their financial life and/or suppressed even if they want to gain financial independence. Thus we have here is a platform which serves as their salvation and a symbol of freedom from a centuries long bondage.

How It Works ?

SheWork prioritizes the welfare of the rural entrepreneur. The open market space is an open playground for buyers, investors and sponsors, to analyse the work of various craftswoman, weavers and so on and so forth. We have a chat application in built in the user's profile to further the discussion with a particular buyer or an investor. We also have introduced the idea pitching page, in which the women who need a rapid growth in their business can confer directly with the experts by pitching the idea of their business. Last but not the least, are the blogs, for the women, by the women. The blogs help all the buyers, sellers, investors and sponsors to express their experience and show the working at the grass root level.

What Do We Offer?

Open Market Space

This market arena helps the rural women in selling their hand-made/crafted products to potential sponsors and investors. The products have their image displayed with a brief description of the product with their lowest acceptable price, and ways to contact the seller (rural women entrepreneur). The open market space is a good place for potential investors or buyers to review products.

Blogs

These are blogs for women by women! The investors/sponsors/general particiapants on this site can write about their experiences with these rural women as entrepreneurs. These blogs are a step to ameliorate the interaction between the different groups of people operating on the site, and also gives an insight about the grass-root level work with rural women entrepreneurs.

Idea Pitching and Interaction

We have developed a form where the rural entreprenurs can submit their ideas as entries to entreprenurship experts, who then can review their ideas, and help them find the best business model for further expansion. This is a great way for the rural women entreprenurs to build their business and expand into uncharted territory. We also have a chat feature in the user's profile to improve interaction.

About Sacchi Saheli

Sacchi Saheli is a platform for promoting handmade or otherwise made products or intellectual properties of rural women, who are budding entrepreneurs.
